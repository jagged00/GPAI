I am Hunter, I am a ""2d artist"" and I know the basics of modding. I am starting a not-too-complex mod, and I'm trying to make a small team of motivated people to create it. This mod aims to be an improvement in the personality traits. Basically what I'm for is to give a more role-playing style of gameplay to the game by making the personality something dynamic where the events make the characters evolve over time. This would be through an implementation of the vanilla system of experience in the traits; if you take some options, you gain XP that leads you in one route or another. In that way, the decisions you make during the game will shape your character's personality. For example St. Agustine of Hippo that was a very lustful, but with effort became chaste. The basic framework of the mod would be open source, so anyone could make their own implementation, but specific content such as traits, events, etc. would be for the exclusive use of all those contributing to the mod, so those participating in the development would be free to implement it in any mod they are developing.   • Scripting ( I need someone to give me a hand handling some events )
  • A Writer ( You will write the localization files and help planning the content )
  • A Cartoonist (To draw 2 or 3 personality trait icons)
