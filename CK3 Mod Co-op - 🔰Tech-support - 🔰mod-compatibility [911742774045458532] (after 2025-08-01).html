YAGPDB.xyz BOT 8/18/2025 4:50 PM
Sometimes the mod crashes and the error log doesn't help. We've all been there. If there's nothing else you can do (like solve any other reported issues, even if they may seem harmless), then you can do a binary search process. First, back up your mod somewhere. Then, select half the files or folders in the mod, and delete them. Start with systems like events, gui and localisation that are on top of the architecture rather than at its core. If you can now launch the mod, then the crash issue was located somewhere in the deleted files. If you still cannot launch it, the issue is in the undeleted half of your files. Having found the half with the problem, repeat the process for that half by deleting one quarter of the mod. Then one eighth, all the way until you can narrow it down to a single file. Then, start commenting out parts of that file until you can resolve it to one entity, and finally one line of script. This process takes some time and many launches of the game, but if nothing else makes sense, it's a good fallback method.
