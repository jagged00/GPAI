Do you want to make a total conversion? Are you excited with an idea for a world, that you want to realise in CK3? And do you want to embark on that project with little prior modding experience? Consider these uncomfortable truths:
  • Total conversions are a terrifying chore to make. To get one going takes months of effort with little return on investment, and countless times where you're scratching your head at an inane vanilla system and spend a day in frustration before you figure it out. In that process, because you are figuring everything out, setbacks will happen again and again.
  • Total conversions are a big effort to maintain. If you've managed to finish one, after every big patch you will be redoing 10% of your mod again. Two or three times a year, you need to spend a few weeks on more dumb chores just to get the thing going as well as it did before the patch. And CK3 likely won't stop getting patches until the 2030s.
  • Total conversions do exist but they are, as a rule, made and maintained by teams. There are no TC mods in recent memory that have been made from scratch by a single person, and trust us, you don't want to be the first.
  • About every other week someone comes to the Coop with a vision for a world, and they ask for help making a map. They don't know what they are getting into, they burn themselves out and stop modding altogether.
Please, for your health and ours, consider very carefully whether you really want to do such a gigantic and thankless task as one of your first projects. CK3 is not a game engine, it is a game, and the larger the scope of your mod the bigger the pain.
